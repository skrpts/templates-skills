## Capability

Reviews any output against a defined quality rubric or checklist. Produces a structured assessment with pass/fail per criterion and improvement suggestions.

## When to Use

- As a review step before finalising any deliverable
- When you have specific quality criteria that must be met
- For peer review simulation in academic or professional contexts

## What It Does

1. **Criterion-by-criterion assessment** — evaluates each quality requirement
2. **Evidence citation** — points to specific parts of the content for each judgement
3. **Gap identification** — what's missing or incomplete
4. **Improvement suggestions** — specific, actionable recommendations
5. **Overall verdict** — pass/needs revision/fail with summary

## Inputs

Content to review and quality criteria or rubric.

## Outputs

Structured quality assessment with per-criterion results and recommendations.

## Capability

Processes meeting transcripts or notes and produces structured summaries: decisions made, action items with owners, key discussion points, and follow-ups.

## When to Use

- After any meeting with a transcript or detailed notes
- When meeting notes need to be distributed to stakeholders
- As input to project tracking workflows

## What It Does

1. **Decision extraction** — identifies decisions made with context
2. **Action items** — extracts tasks with assignees and deadlines
3. **Discussion summary** — key points per agenda item
4. **Parking lot** — topics raised but deferred
5. **Attendee contributions** — who said what (if identifiable)

## Inputs

Meeting transcript, recording notes, or detailed meeting notes.

## Outputs

Structured meeting summary with decisions, actions, key points, and follow-ups.

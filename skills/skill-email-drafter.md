## Capability

Drafts emails based on the context, recipient, and intended outcome. Handles formal, informal, follow-up, and cold outreach styles.

## When to Use

- When you need to draft an email and want to get the tone right
- For follow-ups, introductions, proposals, or status updates
- When adapting tone for different audiences (executives, peers, clients)

## What It Does

1. **Tone calibration** — matches formality to recipient and context
2. **Structure** — subject line, opening, body, CTA, sign-off
3. **Length optimisation** — concise for busy recipients, detailed when needed
4. **Follow-up handling** — references previous conversations appropriately

## Inputs

Topic, recipient context, and desired outcome.

## Outputs

Complete email draft with subject line.
